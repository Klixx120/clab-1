The Rae Family

Three generations of loving service and unbeatable food

It all began in 1963 when Abuela emmegrated to Orem, Utah. She wanted a place where everyone could come together and enjoy a taste of her culture. She worked for years and finally saved enough to open a small restaurant "Maria's Tacos".

Since then, her children and grand children have carried on her dream of serving the best mexican food with the love she always carried with her. We strive to keep our flavors and methods authentic so you have the best experience each time you visit.
